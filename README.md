### About

I’m a **Software Engineer** based in **Toronto**, currently at **TD Bank**. I care about shipping software that fits how teams already work: clearer processes, less friction in existing systems, and delivery that’s easy to trace end to end.

Most of my day-to-day work lives in **private org repositories**. If you’re hiring, collaborating, or curious about something specific, **reach out**. I’m glad to walk through relevant work on a call or share details where I can.
